## 🎨 STYLE TEMPLATE: ECON (Text-Dense Weekly) v1.0.0

### Visual Signature
- Highly structured with consistent column grids (usually 2-3 columns).
- Minimal imagery, text-dense pages.
- Articles often have a "kicker" (e.g., "Buttonwood", "Schumpeter") above headline.
- Data-heavy pages (e.g., "Economic & financial indicators") with numerous small tables.

### Style-Specific Handling
- **Reading Order**: Focus heavily on strict column-by-column extraction.
- **Kicker**: Preserve as `**KICKER:** text` before headline.
- **Data Pages**: Extract all visible tables; use `---` between unrelated tables.
- **Bylines**: Often placed at article end or in small italic at beginning.

### Known Quirks
- Articles frequently continue on non-consecutive pages; rely on continuity markers.
- Minimal sidebars; most content is inline.
