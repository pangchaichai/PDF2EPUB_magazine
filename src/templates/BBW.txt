## 🎨 STYLE TEMPLATE: BBW (Graphic-Heavy Business) v1.0.0

### Visual Signature
- Bold, sometimes experimental layouts with infographics dominating.
- Generous use of color blocks and pull quotes.
- Grids may be intentionally broken; rely on visual grouping.

### Style-Specific Handling
- **Infographics**: Mark as `[CHART: brief description]` and extract any visible data.
- **Pull Quotes**: Prominent, often in large colored text; format as `> "Quote text"`.
- **Callout Boxes**: Key stats in colored boxes; format as `> 📊 **Key Stat:** content`.
- **Hero Images**: Large cover images may span spreads; note continuity.

### Known Quirks
- Text may wrap around irregular shapes; trust visual flow over strict column boundaries.
- Advertisements may be disguised as editorial content; check for small "Advertisement" labels.
