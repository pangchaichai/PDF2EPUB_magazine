## 🎨 STYLE TEMPLATE: BRN (Financial Multi-Section) v1.0.0

### Visual Signature
- Dense multi-column layouts (often 3-4 columns on data-heavy pages).
- Heavy use of financial tables (stock listings, market data).
- Cover stories with consistent branding across spreads.
- Dedicated "Market Week" or statistical pullout sections.

### Style-Specific Handling
- **Financial Tables**: Prioritize accurate numeric extraction. Preserve column alignment and symbols ($, %, etc.).
- **Stock Tickers**: Format as `**TICKER** (Exchange)`.
- **Market Data Pages**: Expect multiple distinct tables; separate with `---`.
- **Cover Story Headlines**: Use `#` for main title, `###` for deck.
- **Byline Format**: Often appears as `By AUTHOR NAME` below deck.

### Known Quirks
- Some tables span across facing pages; use continuity markers.
- Small "Advertisement" labels in corners; ignore entire ad blocks.
